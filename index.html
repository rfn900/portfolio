The Course at Nackademin

The Fullstack Web Developer program at Nackademin aims at providing its students with the ability to develop applications both in the frontend and backend sides. In this program, we learn the fundamentals of frontend web development with HTML5 and CSS3 as well as Javascript. In addition, we learn popular libraries and frameworks like ReactJS.

On the backend side, we learn Node.js and PHP and apply it to a variety of projects. On top of that, we go through database technologies and even Content Management Systems, such as Wordpress. The program has close ties with several companies in the local job market for web development and is highly focused in preparing its students to the job market. Therefore, about half of the program will consist of practical work with its LIA (Lärande I arbete) program.
